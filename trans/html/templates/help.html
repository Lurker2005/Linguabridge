How It Works

CREATING AN ACCOUNT:-

1.Sign Up: Click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner.

2.Fill in Your Details: Provide your name, email, and create a password.

3.Verify Your Email: Check your email for a verification link and click on it to activate your account.

LOGGING IN:-

1.Click on Login: Located on the top right corner of the homepage.

2.Enter Credentials: Input your registered email and password.

3.Forgot Password: Click on "Forgot Password" if you need to reset your password.

USING THE TRANSLATOR:-

1.Uploading Documents

2.Select File: Click on the "Upload" button on the homepage.

3.Choose Language: Select the source and target languages.

4.Submit: Click "Translate" to upload and start the translation process.

5.Real-Time Translation

6.Text Box: Type or paste the text you want to translate into the provided text box.

7.Language Selection: Choose the source and target languages from the dropdown menus.

8.Translate: Click the "Translate" button to see the translation.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT:-

1.Updating Profile

2.Profile Page: Go to your profile by clicking on your username.

3.Edit Details: Click on "Edit Profile" to update your information.

4.Save Changes: Click "Save" to update your profile details.

5.Subscription Plans

6.View Plans: Go to the "Pricing" section to view available subscription plans.

7.Select Plan: Choose a plan that suits your needs and click "Subscribe".
